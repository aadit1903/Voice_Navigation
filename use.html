How to Use the Extension

  1. Step 1: Include the extension from your local system.
  2. Step 2: Allow the .crx extension in your browser settings.
  3. Step 3: Pin the extension to your browser's toolbar for easy access.
  4. Step 4: Click on the extension icon to activate it.
  5. Step 5: To open any website, simply say "open."
  6. Step 6: Then, say it loud...
Now, if you want to click on something, just say "CLICK." A set of numerics will appear as an element picker, allowing you to interact with elements on the page effortlessly.

Enjoy using our extension for minor project educational purposes!
